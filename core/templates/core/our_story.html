Our Story

How a swipe right and a hot take led to forever

The Match

It all started with a swipe right on Hinge. Two people, miles apart, both looking for something real in the digital dating world. Little did we know that this simple gesture would change everything.

The Conversation

After matching, we chatted for a while and decided to meet up for our first date. Everything was going well with normal getting-to-know-you conversation...

The First Date Disaster

Then, right there on our first date, Ian decided to break the ice with what can only be described as the worst pickup line in dating history.

So, I have to say something...
I thought your hair would be brighter

*Record scratch* *Freeze frame* Yep, that's Ian making fun of Steph's hair on their FIRST DATE. The audacity.

The Miracle

Against all odds, and probably defying every dating rule ever written, Steph didn't immediately get up and leave. In fact, she found his brutal honesty... refreshing?
