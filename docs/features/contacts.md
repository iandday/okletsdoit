# Contacts

## Structure

### Contact
A Contact is the record of anyone involved in the delivery of your wedding and tracks the following data:
    * Name
    * Company
    * Email Address
    * Phone Number
    * Website
    * Category
    * Notes
    * Record Metadata
      * Created by Name & Timestamp
      * Last Updated by Name & Timestamp
